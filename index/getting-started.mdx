# Getting Started

This guide walks you through connecting to General Market and buying your first Index Tracking Product (ITP) in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

You need:
- A browser wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, or any EVM-compatible wallet)
- USDC on the Index L3 network

## Step 1: Install a Wallet

If you do not already have a browser wallet, install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/download/). Create a new wallet or import an existing one.

<Tip>
Rabby Wallet and other EVM-compatible wallets also work with General Market. Any wallet that supports custom networks will do.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Add the Index L3 Network

General Market runs on an Arbitrum Orbit L3 chain. Add it to your wallet with these details:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Network Name** | Index L3 |
| **RPC URL** | `http://142.132.164.24/` |
| **Chain ID** | `111222333` |
| **Currency Symbol** | ETH |
| **Block Explorer** | -- |

<Tip>
When you visit [generalmarket.io](https://generalmarket.io) and connect your wallet, the app will prompt you to add the Index L3 network automatically. You can skip the manual setup.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Get USDC on Index L3

USDC is the settlement currency for all ITP trades on General Market. To get USDC on the Index L3 network:

1. **Bridge from Arbitrum** -- Use the built-in bridge at [generalmarket.io](https://generalmarket.io) to move USDC from Arbitrum One to Index L3.
2. **Direct transfer** -- If you already have USDC on Index L3, you are ready to go.

<Warning>
USDC on Index L3 uses **18 decimals** (not the standard 6). The bridge handles this conversion automatically. Do not send USDC directly to the L3 contract address -- always use the bridge interface.
</Warning>

## Step 4: Browse Available ITPs

Navigate to the **Markets** tab on General Market. The Markets tab lists all available ITPs, each showing:

- **Name and ticker** -- e.g., "Top 100 Crypto" (TOP100)
- **NAV (Net Asset Value)** -- the current price per share, calculated from underlying asset prices
- **24h change** -- how the NAV has moved in the last 24 hours
- **Composition** -- the assets and weights that make up the ITP
- **Total supply** -- how many shares are currently outstanding

Click on any ITP to view its full details, including the complete asset breakdown and historical NAV chart.

## Step 5: Buy Your First ITP

Once you have selected an ITP to buy:

1. Click **Buy** on the ITP card or detail page.
2. Enter the USDC amount you want to spend.
3. Review the estimated shares you will receive (based on current NAV).
4. Click **Confirm** and approve the transaction in your wallet.

<Info>
The buy order goes through the full [order lifecycle](/index/concepts/order-lifecycle): the order is batched by issuer nodes, executed on a centralized exchange, and ITP tokens are minted to the buyer's wallet. This process typically completes within a few seconds.
</Info>

The transaction involves two wallet interactions:

1. **Approve** -- Allow the contract to spend your USDC (first time only, or if allowance is insufficient).
2. **Submit Order** -- Place the buy order. The USDC is escrowed until the order fills.

## Step 6: Track Your Portfolio

After the buy order fills, ITP tokens appear in the **Portfolio** section. From the Portfolio section you can:

- View your current holdings and their USDC value
- Track profit/loss per position
- Sell any position back to USDC
- Deposit ITP tokens into [lending markets](/index/concepts/lending) to earn yield or borrow against them
