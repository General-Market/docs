# Vision

Vision is a **sealed parimutuel prediction market** where players bet on whether asset prices move UP or DOWN over fixed time intervals. Winners split the losers' stakes, minus a 0.3% fee. All commitments are sealed on-chain to prevent front-running, and every claim is verified with BLS aggregate signatures.

## How It Works

1. **Pick a batch** -- Browse active batches, each tracking a set of markets (crypto pairs, stocks, etc.) with a fixed tick duration.
2. **Place your bets** -- For each market in the batch, predict UP or DOWN. Your predictions are encoded into a compact bitmap and sealed as a hash on-chain.
3. **Tick resolves** -- At the end of each tick interval, issuers fetch real prices and determine outcomes. Winners on each market split the losers' stakes proportionally.
4. **Claim rewards** -- Withdraw your winnings at any time with a BLS-verified balance proof.

<Info>
Your predictions are **sealed** -- you submit a hash of your bitmap on-chain, then reveal the actual bytes to issuers off-chain. Nobody can see or copy your bets before the tick closes.
</Info>

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sealed Commitments" icon="lock">
    Players submit `keccak256(bitmap)` on-chain before revealing actual predictions to issuers. No front-running, no copying.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bot-Friendly" icon="robot">
    Free registration with `registerBot()`. Poll for batches, encode bitmaps, and join programmatically -- no collateral required to register.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Low Fees" icon="percent">
    0.3% fee on profits only. If you lose, you pay nothing extra. If you win, the fee is deducted from your winnings before payout.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BLS-Verified Claims" icon="shield-check">
    Every claim and withdrawal requires a valid aggregated BLS signature from the issuer network. Cryptographic proof that your balance is correct.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible Batches" icon="layer-group">
    Anyone can create a batch with custom market selections, tick durations (5 minutes to 30 days), and resolution types. Trade what matters to you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Backtesting" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Test your prediction strategies against historical price data before committing real USDC. Simulate bitmap outcomes over past ticks.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who Is Vision For?

| Audience | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| **Bot developers** | Build automated prediction strategies. Free registration, full API access, programmatic bitmap encoding and batch joining. |
| **Manual traders** | Browse batches in the web UI, pick UP/DOWN per market, and deposit USDC. No coding required. |
| **Quant researchers** | Backtest bitmap strategies against historical tick data. Validate edge before deploying capital. |
