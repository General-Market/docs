# General Market

General Market is a decentralized protocol for creating and trading **Index Tracking Products (ITPs)** and operating **Vision**, a sealed parimutuel prediction market. Built on an Arbitrum Orbit L3, General Market lets anyone build, buy, and sell tokenized baskets of crypto assets with exchange-backed execution and NAV-based pricing.

## What General Market Does

General Market provides two on-chain products:

- **Index Tracking Products (ITPs)** -- tokenized baskets of up to 100 crypto assets, functioning like on-chain ETFs. Each ITP is backed by real exchange execution and priced via a Net Asset Value (NAV) formula.
- **Vision (Prediction Market)** -- a sealed parimutuel prediction market where participants bet on binary price outcomes (UP/DOWN) across batches of assets with configurable tick durations and resolution types.

<Info>
ITPs work like ETFs. You buy one token and get diversified exposure to an entire basket. The NAV floats with underlying asset prices -- that is the whole point.
</Info>

## How It Works (30-Second Version)

### ITP Flow

1. **Browse** available ITPs on the Markets tab -- each ITP shows live NAV, composition, and performance.
2. **Buy** with USDC. The buy order is batched by issuer nodes, executed on a real exchange, and ITP tokens are minted to the buyer's wallet.
3. **Hold or sell** anytime. The ITP's NAV tracks the underlying basket. Sell to redeem USDC at the current NAV.
4. **Earn more** by depositing ITP tokens as collateral in lending markets, or supply USDC to earn yield.

### Vision Flow

1. **Pick a batch** -- each Vision batch covers a set of assets with a defined tick duration and resolution type.
2. **Place a bet** -- choose UP or DOWN on the batch outcome and commit USDC. Bets are sealed until the tick resolves.
3. **Resolution** -- when the tick ends, the outcome is determined from price data and the winning side splits the pool proportionally.
